Welcome

At our bank, we provide fast and convinient banking services to Singaporeans throughout the country. Some of our services include:
  • Deposit taking
  • Cheque services
  • Loans

We offer a range of account types, some of which includes:

Saving Accounts

Safekeep your money at our banks with a Savings Account. By starting one, you will be able to deposit or withdraw money from our bank anytime.
Grow your savings with us through time with the interest our Savings Accounts provide.
Monthly bank statements will also be provided for you to monitor your spendings and transactions.

Current Accounts

If you frequently withdraw or deposit cash into your account, a current account would be best suited for you.
It provides the same services as a savings account without interest but you will be able to create cheques for transactional purposes.
Monthly bank statements will still be provided for you.
